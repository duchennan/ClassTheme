WLOP

The creator of the original Chinese cartoons Ghost Blade, Cloud Insect, and Dream, and the great touch of digital art painting through self-study

WLOP, whose real name is Wang Ling, graduated from the Department of Information Engineering of the University of Hong Kong, is a self-taught digital art painter, and is affectionately called by netizens as an illustrator writing code. In 2014, he began to create the popular original comic Ghost Blade in his spare time.
